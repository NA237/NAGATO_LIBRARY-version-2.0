Rich father, poor father

BOOK DESCRIPTION


Rich Dad, Poor Dad" is a book written by Robert T. Kiyosaki. He discusses the difference in financial mentality between his his own father, who was an educated but poor man, and his best friend's father, who was less educated but financially wealthy. Kiyosaki shares the lessons he learned from both men about how to think and act to financial freedom. The book offers practical advice on money management, investing and wealth building. It is a very popular book that has influenced many people in their approach to money and personal finance.

Babylon's richest man

BOOK DESCRIPTION


The Richest Man in Babylon" is a personal and financial development book written by George S. Clason. First published in 1926, this book has become a classic in the field of financial management and personal enrichment. Set in the ancient city of Babylon, the story features characters seeking to improve their financial situation by following the advice of Arkad, the richest man in town. Arkad shares his secrets of success and financial principles with his fellow citizens, teaching them the fundamental rules of money and financial management. The book addresses key concepts such as the importance of saving, investing, controlling expenses, managing risk and acquiring financial knowledge. Through simple yet powerful parables and anecdotes, the author conveys timeless lessons on how to create and preserve wealth.

my mother's goat

BOOK DESCRIPTION


La chèvre de ma mère" is a book written by Ricardo Kaniama, a Congolese writer and director. Published in 2018, this autobiographical book tells the story of the author, who grew up in a remote village in the Democratic Republic of Congo, raised by his mother and surrounded by his siblings. The book's title refers to the goat owned by the author's mother, which was a source of pride and sustenance for the family. Through touching and poignant anecdotes, the author shares his childhood marked by poverty and daily challenges, but also by his mother's unconditional love. Ricardo Kaniama sensitively describes the difficulties his family had to face, but also the moments of joy, solidarity and hope that marked his journey. He evokes the strength and resilience of his mother, who sacrificed everything to offer her children a better life despite the difficult circumstances. This poignant tale highlights family values, the importance of love and determination, and everyone's ability to overcome obstacles to realize their dreams. "La chèvre de ma mère" is a tribute to the author's mother, her Congolese roots and the strength of the human spirit in the face of adversity. This book is recommended for those who appreciate authentic, moving stories, as well as for those interested in African culture and history.

AN ATYPICAL DOCTOR

BOOK DESCRIPTION


Un médecin atypique" is a book written by Dr. Bernard Fontanille, a French doctor specializing in traditional and alternative medicine. Published in 2016, this autobiographical book recounts the professional and personal journey of the author, who has chosen an unconventional approach to medicine. In this book, Dr. Fontanille shares his experience as a traveling physician, exploring different cultures and medical traditions around the world. He recounts his encounters with local healers, shamans, practitioners of traditional Chinese and Ayurvedic medicine and other ancestral medical approaches. Through captivating stories and inspiring anecdotes, the author highlights the importance of the diversity of medical practices and underlines the effectiveness of certain alternative methods in treating illness and improving patients' well-being. He also discusses the challenges he has faced as an atypical doctor, challenging established norms and seeking innovative solutions for his patients. "An Atypical Doctor" offers an original look at medicine and healthcare, inviting readers to rethink their vision of health and care. This book is recommended for those interested in alternative medicine, discovering other medical cultures and exploring new therapeutic approaches.
